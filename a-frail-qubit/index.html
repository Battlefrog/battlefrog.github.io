A Frail Qubit


Steam

You control a Qubit, an Artifically Intelligent Quantum Bit, deep within a Learning Interface of a Quantum Computer, completing Trials of intelligence, speed, and patience.

The Qubit is not alone.

Encounter the Sage, a Superintelligent, all knowing entity who has mastered the Infinite, and is now here to guide you.

Talk with various employees of Bree Inc, a Corporation that will harness the power of AI no matter the cost, through Distortions in the Learning Interface.

The Qubit is frail.

The Trials are not a pleasant place for the Qubit, and death is always around. From Black Holes to Shooters, Lasers and Bombs, to the Walls that surround the Trials themselves, hazards are everywhere.

Features

  • A Story Mode with many Trials, twists and turns, and multiple endings.
  • An Arcade Mode to hone your skills and get the best times on the global Steam Leaderboard.
